# Fix "Safety filters blocked this generation" Error

## What This Means

Gemini's safety filters are blocking the image generation because the content might violate Google's safety policies.

## Solutions

### Solution 1: Modify Your Prompt

Make your prompt more specific and professional:

**Instead of:**
- "scary person" 
- "violent scene"
- Vague descriptions

**Try:**
- "Professional business person in a modern office setting"
- "Cinematic landscape with dramatic lighting"
- "Professional product photography with clean background"

### Solution 2: Be More Descriptive

Add more detail about what you want:

**Bad:**
- "person with weapon"

**Good:**
- "Professional actor in costume holding a prop, cinematic lighting, movie poster style"

### Solution 3: Avoid Sensitive Topics

Avoid prompts related to:
- Violence or weapons
- Adult content
- Controversial topics
- Medical/health claims
- Political content

### Solution 4: Try Different Variations

The app generates 3 variations. If one is blocked, try:
- A different variation
- A different style
- A different prompt entirely

## Tips for Better Prompts

1. **Be specific**: "Professional YouTube thumbnail of a tech product on a clean white background"
2. **Add style**: "Cinematic lighting, professional photography, high quality"
3. **Be appropriate**: Keep content suitable for all audiences
4. **Describe composition**: "Close-up shot, vibrant colors, high contrast"

## If Still Blocked

1. Try a completely different prompt
2. Simplify your concept
3. Focus on objects/scenes rather than people
4. Use more professional/technical language
